As a student of Computer Science and programmer by trade, I often find ways to associate the projects I work on with programming. This resume is an attempt to present the skills that I hope to use in the Computer Science field alongside my passion for programming. 'Hello World!' is every programmer's first step into a new language, and I thought it appropriate to use the concept for my first step into web development. Each page is modeled after the 'Hello World' program for one of the languages I learned during my studies at Indiana University.
